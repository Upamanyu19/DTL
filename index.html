About MedWaste Connect

The rapid expansion of clinical facilities requires an equally robust framework for biomedical waste management. MedWaste Connect bridges the gap between healthcare providers and authorized disposal vendors.

Our platform acts as a secure aggregator, ensuring that hazardous materials—from pharmacological waste to sharps—are tracked, managed, and disposed of according to strict environmental guidelines.

Biomedical Waste Segregation

Strict adherence to molecular and toxicological safety standards begins at the source.

Yellow Category

Contents: Human anatomical waste, animal waste, soiled waste, expired/discarded medicines, and microbiology waste.

Red Category

Contents: Contaminated waste (recyclable) such as tubing, intravenous sets, catheters, syringes (without needles).

White Category (Sharps)

Contents: Needles, scalpels, blades, or any contaminated sharp object causing puncture cuts.

Blue Category

Contents: Broken or discarded glass, vials, and ampoules.
